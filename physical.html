Physical Wealth

 

Definition:

Physical wealth is the foundational, compounding, and sustainable state of having high-level health, energy, and physical vitality.

 

How to achieve physical wealth

Achieving physical wealth requires consistent, simple daily habits: moving your body for at least 45 minutes, eating nutrient-dense, whole foods, and securing 7–9 hours of quality sleep.
