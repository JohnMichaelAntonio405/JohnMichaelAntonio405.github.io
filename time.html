Time Wealth

 

Definition:

Time wealth is the ability to control your own schedule, prioritizing personal freedom, autonomy, and meaningful activities over constant work or money accumulation.

 

How to achieve time wealth

Achieving time wealth requires treating time as your most precious asset, automating or outsourcing tasks, and setting strict boundaries.
