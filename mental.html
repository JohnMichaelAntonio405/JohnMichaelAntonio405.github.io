Mental Wealth

 

Definition:

Mental wealth is the abundance of psychological, emotional, and cognitive resources that allow individuals and communities to thrive, rather than just survive.

 

How to achieve mental wealth

Achieving mental wealth involves building emotional resilience, cognitive capital, and a positive mindset through daily habits like mindfulness, exercise, and lifelong learning.
