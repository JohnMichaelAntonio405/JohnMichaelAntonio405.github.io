Wealth

 

Definition:

Wealth is the accumulation of valuable resources, assets, and money, often defined as total assets minus liabilities (net worth).

Wealth Types

The five core pillars essential for a fulfilling life are:

 

  • Time Wealth: The ability to choose how, where, and with whom you spend your time.

    •  

  • Social Wealth: Deep, meaningful bonds and a strong network of family, friends, and community.

    •  

  • Physical Wealth: A sense of purpose, continuous learning, and inner peace, rather than constant stress.

    •  

  • Mental Wealth: The physical health and energy to fully enjoy life.

    •  

  • Financial Wealth: Sufficient assets to support your desired lifestyle and provide security.

    •  

How to achieve wealth

Achieving wealth requires a balanced, long-term approach to life, focusing on building assets in five key areas: Time, Social, Mental, Physical, and Financial.
