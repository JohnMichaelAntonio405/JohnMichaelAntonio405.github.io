Financial Wealth

 

Definition:

Financial wealth is the total value of an individual's or household's assets (cash, investments, property) minus their liabilities (debts, mortgages) at a specific point in time.

 

How to achieve financial wealth

Building financial wealth involves a disciplined combination of earning more, spending less, and investing the difference consistently over time.
